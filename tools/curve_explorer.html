Curve Explorer — combat formula sketchpad

This is the throwaway calculator from the SCB plan (prep item 4) — not the real combat engine. It implements combat_formula_spec.md: the growth curve, stat budgets, and the fight pipeline, then fights thousands of simulated battles per checkpoint level. Change any constant below and press Recompute — every table re-derives instantly. Colored badges show whether each result hits the targets in balance_targets_concept.md.

Reading guide: the Reference Fight row group is the main event — a fully geared hero vs. an on-level enemy. Targets: kill time 10–20s, HP cost ~25% per kill, win rate 100%. The Naked table should show near-losses (gear is vital, by design). Punching Up should get expensive fast.

1 · Reference Fight — geared hero vs. on-level enemy

Volatility passes when 90% of fights finish within ±30% of the median kill time (target T13). Food/kill = HP lost × 1.5 safety margin — what the kitchen must supply per fight for a sustainable chain (T6).

2 · Naked hero vs. on-level enemy — "gear is vital" check

Intent (targets doc §3 / spec §3): a naked on-level fight is a coin flip or worse. If naked heroes win comfortably here, gear isn't vital enough.

3 · Punching up — geared hero vs. enemies above their level

Owner target (D2): a well-geared hero squeezes out a few kills at +10–20% relative level; beyond that it turns lethal. "Kills (no heal)" = median kills before dying with zero recovery.
