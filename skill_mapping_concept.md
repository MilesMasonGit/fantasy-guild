# Skill Mechanics Concept Document: Consolidated Skills

This document defines the consolidated list of **11 skills**, mapping all **21 core loop mechanics** into this refined structure. Each skill now houses a primary thematic mechanic, a complementary secondary mechanic, and an optional tertiary mechanic to support advanced synergies.

---

## 1. Defining the Three Basic Task Types

To ensure every skill has a clear path for leveling up and interacting with the game economy, all repeatable cards in the loop are categorized into three **Basic Task Types**:

### A. Gathering Tasks (Raw Resource Generation)
*   **Description:** Tasks that generate raw resources from the environment for zero material cost (consuming only hero time or energy).
*   **Gameplay Purpose:** They are the foundation of the guild economy, injecting raw materials (ore, wood, fish, herbs) into the global bank.
*   **Example:** *Mine Iron Vein* (Action Card) — Takes 12 seconds, consumes 2 Energy, produces 1x Iron Ore.

### B. Processing Tasks (Item Conversion)
*   **Description:** Tasks or station recipes that take inputs from the bank and convert them into different, higher-tier items.
*   **Gameplay Purpose:** Transforming raw materials into finished equipment, potions, foods, or tools.
*   **Example:** *Smelt Iron Ingot* (Station Recipe) — Consumes 3x Iron Ore and 1x Coal to craft 1x Iron Ingot.

### C. Consumption Tasks (Material Sinks & Advantages)
*   **Description:** Tasks that actively consume items specifically to yield Gold, XP, high-tier status buffs, or loop skips.
*   **Gameplay Purpose:** The ultimate "sinks" of the economy, converting physical items into game-progression advantages.
*   **Example:** *Lemonade Stand* (Action Card) — Consumes 1x Lemonade to produce 15 Gold and 10 Cooking XP.

## 2. Skill Domain Registry & Task Focus

Below is the ledger of the 11 skills, detailing their primary, secondary, and tertiary mechanics, as well as their primary leveling task focus.

---

### 1. Science
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Adjacency (Positional Synergy)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Task Haste (Duration Reduction)
*   **Tertiary Mechanic:** Passive Status Immunity (Negation - *e.g., shielding/filtering hazards*)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Consumption & Processing**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** The mastery of layouts, efficiency, infrastructure, and technology. Science focuses on optimizing the deck structure (adjacency) and building logistical speed upgrades (roads, pipelines) or defensive hazard shields to protect heroes.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Belt Assembly* (Outpost Station - Adjacency): Boosts the execution speed of any station placed next to it by +15%.
    *   *Cobblestone Roads* (Outpost Station - Task Haste): Reduces the task duration of all Labor/Mining tasks in this area's loop by 10%.
    *   *Hazmat Filter* (Passive Upgrade - Passive Status Immunity): Protects the hero from gas or poison hazard cards in the area loop.

### 2. Labor
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Yield Multiplication (Ore Output)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Rare Table Rollover (Special Drops)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Gathering**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Subterranean exploration, quarrying, and extracting raw geological treasures. Labor covers mining veins, digging up fossils, clearing rock hazards, and unearthing rare gems from the earth.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Heavy Pickaxe* (Active Tool - Yield Multiplication): Grants a +25% chance to double ore harvested.
    *   *Gem Prospecting* (Action Card - Rare Table Rollover): A task card with a 15% chance to roll on the *Precious Gems* table instead of standard stone.

### 3. Forge
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Equipment Smithing (Weapons & Armor Fabrication)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Passive Status Immunity (Negation)
*   **Tertiary Mechanic:** Upkeep Discount (Material Preservation - *PROPOSED: Scrap Recycling*)
*   *Added Utility:* Utility Engineering (Tools & Traversal Devices Construction)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Processing**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Shaping and fortifying metal armaments at the furnace. Forge governs weapon making, defensive plate production, crafting mechanical traversal tools (ropes, ladders), and conserving ores through recycling and material thrift.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Plate Forging* (Station Recipe - Equipment Smithing): Crafts an *Iron Breastplate*.
    *   *Insulated Plating* (Passive Upgrade - Passive Status Immunity): Adds fire immunity to forged armor, protecting against lava hazards.
    *   *Workshop: Rope* (Station Recipe - Utility Engineering): Crafts 1x *Rope* from fiber inputs.

### 4. Aquatic
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Yield Multiplication (Aquatic Output)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Rare Table Rollover (Aquatic Special Drops)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Gathering**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Managing resources, traversal, and hazards relating to water bodies (rivers, oceans, lakes). Aquatic specializes in maximizing fish harvests, navigating deep water channels, and hauling up rare sunken cargo.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Fishing Rod* (Active Tool - Yield Multiplication): Grants a chance to double fish catches.
    *   *Deep Water Trawl* (Action Card - Rare Table Rollover): A task card with a chance to roll on the *Sunken Treasure* table instead of returning standard fish.

### 5. Nature
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Passive Generation (Unassigned Productivity)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Upkeep Discount (Material Preservation)
*   **Tertiary Mechanic:** Adjacency (Positional Synergy - *e.g., symbiotic ecosystem layouts*)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Gathering & Passive Generation**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Managing ecological and organic operations, agriculture, and foraging. Nature covers crop growth, woodcutting, wild plants, animal pens, and arranging crops in symbiotic ecosystem patterns to boost adjacent growth.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Chicken Coop* (Outpost Station - Passive Generation): Passively deposits 1x Egg into the bank every 30 seconds with no hero assigned.
    *   *Seed Sift* (Passive Upgrade - Upkeep Discount): Grants a 30% chance to preserve seeds when planting crops.
    *   *Symbiotic Crop* (Card - Adjacency): Boosts the harvest yield of any adjacent *Grain* or *Berry* cards by +10%.

### 6. Cooking
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Provisioning (Food & Drink Production)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Consumable Restoration (Vitals & Buff Absorption)
*   **Tertiary Mechanic:** Active Status Cleansing (Purging - *e.g., drinks/soups curing debuffs*)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Processing & Consumption**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Preparing nourishing food and eating it for optimal efficiency. Cooked food heals massive amounts of health and energy, while warm broths and curative teas cleanse active poisons or colds.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Bake Blackberry Pie* (Station Recipe - Provisioning): Crafts 1x *Blackberry Pie*.
    *   *Feast Goblet* (Action Card - Consumable Restoration): Consumes a meal, boosting its healing and status duration by +20%.
    *   *Curative Broth* (Action Card - Active Status Cleansing): Consumes 1x Hot Soup to purge the *Frozen* or *Chilled* debuffs.

### 7. Occult
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Enchanting (Runes & Magic Augmentation)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Card Mutator / Transmutation (Queue Override)
*   **Tertiary Mechanic:** Cross-Area Telemetry (Synchronized Guild Statuses - *PROPOSED: Leyline Rituals*)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Processing & Consumption**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** reality-bending magic and mystical craftsmanship. The Occult focuses on infusing weapons/armor with runes and broadcasting magic global buffs via ritual circles.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Carve Rune of Defense* (Station Recipe - Enchanting): Crafts a defensive rune.
    *   *Transmute Ore* (Action Card - Card Mutator): Mutates the next raw metal node to yield gold instead of iron.
    *   *Blood Tithe* (Action Card - Mutator): Lose 20 HP, gain 2 HP per draw for the loop.

### 8. Alchemy
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Alchemy (Potions & Elixirs Synthesis)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Active Status Cleansing (Purging)
*   **Tertiary Mechanic:** Card Mutator / Transmutation (Queue Override - *e.g., chemical transmutations*)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Processing & Consumption**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Compounding herbs and chemical reagents into liquid curatives. Alchemists brew powerful potions designed to immediately purge poison, curses, and bleeding, or use reactive acids to mutate barriers.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Distill Antidote* (Station Recipe - Alchemy): Synthesizes a potion that cleanses poisons.
    *   *Quaff Curative* (Action Card - Active Status Cleansing): Instantly consumes an antidote potion to purge any active damage-over-time status effects.
    *   *Acid Spray* (Action Card - Card Mutator): Uses 1x Acid to dissolve and mutate the next *Rock Blockade* card into a blank slot.

### 9. Crime
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Keyed Loot Extraction (Locked Chest Drops)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Sneak Pass (Agility-Based Avoidance)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Consumption**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Stealth, locks, and illegal extraction. Crime specializes in using lockpicks on treasure chests and slipping past hazards or combat encounters undetected.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Pick Lock* (Action Card - Keyed Loot Extraction): Consumes 1x *Lockpick* to roll on a rare chest table.
    *   *Shadow Step* (Passive Check - Sneak Pass): Grants a 25% chance to slip past patrol combat slots without fighting.

### 10. Explore
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Resource-Based Mapping (Deterministic Skip)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Reusable Restoration (Camping & Tents)
*   **Tertiary Mechanic:** Card/Slot Injection (Queue Expansion - *e.g., discovering secret paths*)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Consumption**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Navigation, pathfinding, and survival. Explore focuses on mapping out unknown territories to plan paths, pitching campsites, and discovering hidden trails that temporarily append extra resource slots to the loop.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Surveyed Route* (Action Card - Resource-Based Mapping): Consumes 1x *Scouting Map* to skip the next hazard.
    *   *Leyline Ward* (Action Card - Reusable Restoration): A campfire or recovery ward in the loop that restores 15 energy for zero resource cost.
    *   *Discover Secret Trail* (Action Card - Card/Slot Injection): Temporarily injects a *Hidden Orchard* resource slot immediately following this card.

### 11. Social
*   **Primary Mechanic:** Card/Slot Injection (Queue Expansion)
*   **Secondary Mechanic:** Cross-Area Telemetry (Synchronized Guild Statuses)
*   **Primary Task Focus:** **Consumption**
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Guild management, trade relations, and leadership. Social is about expanding the loop size by attracting merchants and broadcasting status boosts to other active heroes across the guild.
*   **Example Cards:**
    *   *Market Stand* (Action Card - Card/Slot Injection): Temporarily injects a trading slot into the loop.
    *   *Guild Banner* (Outpost Station - Cross-Area Telemetry): Stationing a hero here boosts the combat damage of all other active heroes by +10%.

## 3. Combat Skill Domain & Stat Derivation

While the 11 production skills govern the Area Deck Loop, the **4 Combat Skills** strictly govern the autonomous combat encounters. The combat style a hero uses during an encounter is entirely determined by the type of weapon they have equipped.

### 12. Melee
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Close-quarters physical combat using swords, axes, and hammers.
*   **Governs:** Scales **Damage** and **Accuracy** when a Melee weapon is equipped.

### 13. Ranged
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Physical combat from a distance using bows, crossbows, and throwing weapons.
*   **Governs:** Scales **Damage** and **Accuracy** when a Ranged weapon is equipped.

### 14. Magic
*   **Theme & Rationale:** Spellcasting and elemental attacks using staves, wands, and tomes.
*   **Governs:** Scales **Damage** and **Accuracy** when a Magic weapon is equipped.

### 15. Defense
*   **Theme & Rationale:** The universal survivability skill. Represents a hero's physical fortitude and ability to utilize armor.
*   **Governs:** Scales base **HP** and increases the effectiveness of equipped **Armor** and **Block** values, regardless of the weapon held.
